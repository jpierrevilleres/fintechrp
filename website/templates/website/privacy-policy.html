Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how FintechRP collects, uses and manages information when visitors access the website. By using the site, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Visitors can choose to subscribe to newsletters. Email addresses provided for this purpose are used only to send updates and may be removed at any time upon request.

Cookies and Tracking

FintechRP uses cookies for analytics, functionality and personalization. Cookies help analyze traffic, understand user behavior and improve site content. Some cookies may come from third party services such as Google Analytics, YouTube embeds, TikTok embeds and affiliate partners.

Users can manage or disable cookies in their browser settings.

Third Party Services

FintechRP may display embedded content like YouTube or TikTok videos and may contain affiliate links. These third parties may collect data according to their own privacy practices. We encourage visitors to review their policies.

Google AdSense

When activated, Google AdSense may use cookies to show personalized or non-personalized ads. This may include the use of DoubleClick cookies. Visitors in regions with data protection laws such as the European Union will be given choices regarding ad preferences.

Visitors may request the deletion of personal data previously submitted to the site. To request deletion contact us at webmaster@fintechrp.com or through the contact page.

Data Protection

We take reasonable steps to protect information from unauthorized access or misuse. However, no digital transmission is completely secure. By using the site you acknowledge this.

GDPR Compliance

Visitors in the European Union may request access, correction or deletion of any personal data stored. All such requests can be sent to webmaster@fintechrp.com.
