Cookie Policy

Last updated: November 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how FintechRP uses cookies and similar technologies.

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to help improve your browsing experience and site functionality.

How We Use Cookies

FintechRP uses cookies for:

  • Basic website functionality
  • Analytics and performance metrics
  • Improving content relevance
  • Supporting embedded media
  • Supporting affiliate partners
  • Supporting advertising services when active such as Google AdSense for personalized and non-personalized ads

Types of Cookies

Visitors may encounter different categories of cookies such as:

Necessary Cookies

Allow the site to function

Analytics Cookies

Used to measure performance

Preference Cookies

Remember user settings

Marketing Cookies

Used by advertising partners like Google AdSense

These categories may apply depending on active integrations.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow visitors to manage or disable cookies. Disabling cookies may affect certain functions of the website.

Cookie Banner

FintechRP uses a cookie banner to inform users in regions that require notification such as the European Union. Visitors can accept or adjust cookie preferences.

Third Party Cookies

Some cookies come from services such as Google Analytics, YouTube, TikTok and affiliate partners. These cookies follow the policies of their respective providers.

Changes to the Cookie Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in technology, services or legal requirements.
